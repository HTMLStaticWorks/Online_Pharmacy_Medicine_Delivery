Frequently Asked Questions

How does the temperature-stabilized shipping process work? ▼
Sensitive medications are packed into vacuum-insulated containers with gel refrigerants. An active IoT sensor monitors internal temperatures constantly, triggering dispatcher alerts if thresholds move out of the 2°C to 8°C limits.
Do you accept prescription documents from clinical GPs outside the country? ▼
No. To maintain regulatory compliance and safety checks, we only process prescriptions signed by doctors registered under national clinical bodies.
Is there an enrollment fee for the Auto-Refill program? ▼
No. Subscribing to auto-refills is completely free. You can pause, cancel, or reschedule upcoming deliveries at any time from your settings panel without incurring any penalty costs.
