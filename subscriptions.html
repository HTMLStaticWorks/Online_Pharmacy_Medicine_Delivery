Subscription FAQ & Help

Find clinical guidance on billing cycles, pauses, and prescription renewal rules.

Can I pause or skip an upcoming auto-refill delivery? ▼
Yes. You can pause, skip, or modify your delivery date for any upcoming refill directly inside your patient dashboard. Changes must be saved at least 24 hours before your scheduled dispatch window.
What happens when my primary GP prescription expires? ▼
Our clinical system tracks your prescription validity. We will send you an email and SMS alert 14 days before expiration so you can request a renewal from your GP or schedule a free review with our prescribing team.
Is there a contract commitment or cancellation fee? ▼
No. The program is fully flexible. There are no contracts, sign-up costs, or cancellation fees. You can end your auto-refill enrollment at any time with a single click in your settings.
