Clinical Use

Atorvastatin is commonly prescribed as an adjunct to diet to reduce elevated total cholesterol, LDL-C, apo B, and TG levels, and to increase HDL-C in patients with primary hypercholesterolemia. Take orally once daily with or without food. Avoid double-dosing if a day is skipped.

Dosing Matrix

The starting dose is typically 10mg or 20mg once daily. Dose ranges are customized from 10mg to 80mg once daily based on blood lipid panels. Check-ups are recommended 2 to 4 weeks after starting dosage changes.

Recorded Side Effects

Common reactions include headache, nausea, diarrhea, muscle aches, and fatigue. If muscle weakness is accompanied by fever, consult a clinical specialist immediately.
